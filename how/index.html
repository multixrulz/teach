How to teach

Teaching is…

Teaching is what happens when one person helps another person to learn.

Let’s analyse that idea.

  1. The other person has to learn something. If the other person isn’t learning, you’re not teaching. You might be going through the motions of doing what teachers do, but teaching isn’t defined by what the teacher does. Teaching is defined by the learning that happens.
  2. The other person has to learn faster with the teacher than they would by themselves. If the person doing the learning could learn just as fast without you, you’re not teaching. If the person doing the learning could learn faster without you, you’re actually doing the opposite of teaching. Teaching is also defined by the efficiency of the learning.

To teach effectively, you really need to know how people learn.
