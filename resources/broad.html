Teaching Websites

…that have a wide variety of resources.

Collections of resources

  • Our Learning SA - South Australian resources for students. They link to lots of other sites.
  • Victorian resources aligned to the curriculum

Libre

  • OpenCulture “Open Culture scours the web for the best educational media. We find the free courses and audio books you need, the language lessons & educational videos you want, and plenty of enlightenment in between.”
  • OER Commons - Open Education Resources - seems to be a place to search for OER materials, no idea how they locate them.
  • Illinois Open Education Resources - Search for OER.
  • Toy Theater - virtual manipulatives and games
  • Class Playground - theory, printables, and interactives for art, literacy and numeracy.

Gratis

  • Teaching Ideas - UK based, plenty of free ideas and links to both free and paid resources (which is presumably how they make their money).
  • Australian Curriculum Resources
  • Weebly - Probably some good stuff. Hard to navigate.
  • Cool Australia - Free resources of all sorts
  • TED Ed

Taxpayer-funded

  • NSW DoE - seems pretty handy
  • ABC education - the Aussie broadcaster

Paid

  • TeachStarter
  • Twinkl
  • The Measured Mom - reading, writing, maths. For year 1-2 for parents and teachers.
  • Matrix - tutoring. They have some useful subject overviews and ideas of what kids find hard, and a few other resources.
